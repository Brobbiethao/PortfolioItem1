About Us


We are certified Yoga instructors with a combined total of 23 years of experience. Our location contains 2 rooms, a standard studio and a bikram sauna room. Additionally, we have an on site shower and lockerooms available.

What makes us different from the rest of the millenial pop-up lifestyle shops? After facility expenses, instructor salary and cash reserves thresholds are met. Excess proceeds are donated to the local women's shelter! How are we able to do this? We charge $20/month(12 month contract) or $10 a class. What you see on the app or on our website is the publicly available health of our business!
